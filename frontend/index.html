Track Your Money and Find Your Budget

Buddy the Budget Tracker is an easy-to-use budgeting tool designed to help you track weekly income, manage everyday spending, and stay on top of savings and debt goals. Instead of complex spreadsheets or overwhelming charts, Buddy gives you a clear snapshot of where your money goes each week, helping you build better habits and feel more in control of your finances. The tracker allows users to organize their finances into simple categories such as income, fixed expenses, variable spending, savings contributions, and debt payments.
